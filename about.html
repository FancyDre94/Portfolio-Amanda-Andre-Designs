About Me

Hello! I have been doing art since I was a child. I think the first memory of myself creating art was when my parents would buy me 'How to Draw' books and I would spend all day trying to recreate the images. Growing up I always looked forward to artclass. Once I was in highschool I took as many as I was allowed, there I found my love for painting. I love to create art that shows what some think are imperfections to be objects of beauty. I want to share my art, of bodies of all shapes and sizes, because this is something I wish I saw growing up. Art has the ability to give confidence to embrace and love your body. I've lived most of my life in the Metro Detriot cities of Michigan. I am now living in the Flint Area. If I'm not spending my free time painting I'm enjoying the local farmers market.

Thank you for taking the time to visit my page.
